About SMA

Our Mission

At School of Martial Arts (SMA), we believe karate is more than physical technique‚Äîit's a path to personal growth. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified institution run by Shri Sathya Sai Educational Trust, our mission is to develop well-rounded individuals through the practice of traditional karate, emphasizing:

  • Discipline in training and daily life
  • Respect for instructors, peers, and self
  • Perseverance through challenges
  • Confidence through achievement
  • Community and mutual support

We create a positive learning environment where students of all ages can discover their potential, build physical fitness, learn practical self-defense, and develop the mental strength that carries beyond the dojo into every aspect of life.

What We Teach

Traditional Karate

Authentic martial arts training covering fundamental techniques, forms, and sparring. Build strength, discipline, and practical self-defense skills through time-tested methods.

Weapon Training

Traditional martial arts weapons training including bo staff, nunchaku, and other classical weapons. Develop coordination, focus, and respect for martial arts heritage.

Jujutsu Training

Japanese grappling and self-defense techniques including throws, joint locks, and ground defense. Complement striking skills with practical close-range combat methods.

Meditation

Mental training and mindfulness practices to develop focus, calmness, and inner strength. Learn techniques to reduce stress and enhance mental clarity.

Yoga

Flexibility, breathing, and body awareness training. Improve range of motion, prevent injuries, and develop mind-body connection essential for martial arts excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No! We welcome complete beginners. Our curriculum starts with basics and progresses systematically.

What should I wear?

Comfortable athletic clothing for your first classes. A karate gi (uniform) can be purchased later.

How long until I can earn my first belt?

Progression varies by individual effort and attendance. Most students test for their first belt advancement within 3-6 months of consistent training.

Are classes safe for children?

Yes. Safety is our priority. Kids learn in a controlled environment with age-appropriate training methods.

Can I start at any time?

Yes! We accept new students year-round. Contact us to arrange your first class.

Is karate good exercise?

Absolutely. Karate provides a full-body workout improving strength, flexibility, endurance, and coordination.

Can seniors train?

Yes! Karate is for all ages. As you can see, karate can benefit you no matter how old you are. These benefits can be physical, mental, or spiritual.
